# Pakistani E-Commerce Data Analysis

End-to-end data analysis of a simulated Pakistani e-commerce store with 2,000 customers, 100 products, and 2,000 orders. Built with Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib.

## Key Business Insights
- **Top Categories**: Electronics & Clothing drive maximum revenue.
- **Payment Behavior**: 65%+ orders use Cash on Delivery - major working capital risk.
- **Regional Focus**: Punjab & Sindh contribute 60%+ of total revenue.
- **Customer Retention**: Customers purchase within ~2 weeks of signup - loyalty programs needed.
